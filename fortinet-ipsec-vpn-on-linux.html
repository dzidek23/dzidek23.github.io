Fortinet IPSEC VPN on Linux

dzidek23

/etc/ipsec.secrets

'vpn username' = XAUTH 0s.....

'vpn machine' = PSK 0s.....

/etc/ipsec.conf

conn fortinet
    left=
    leftauth=psk
    leftid=""
    leftauth2=xauth
    xauth_identity="vpn username"
    leftsourceip=%config
    right='VPN IP'
    rightsubnet='Internal IP subnet'/24
    rightauth=psk
    keyexchange=ikev1
    aggressive=yes
    ike=aes128-sha1-modp1536!
    esp=aes128-sha1-modp1536!
    auto=add

More about IPSec secrets here: https://wiki.strongswan.org/projects/strongswan/wiki/IpsecSecrets
